Mailinglists

LessTif currently has two public mailing lists: lesstif, and lesstif-commits. Those are used to keep in touch, solve problems, and track development.

LessTif Public Mailing Lists
lesstif@lesstif.org is the list for general discussion of LessTif.
Visit http://terror.hungry.com/mailman/listinfo/lesstif (or https version) to subscribe.
lesstif-commits shows CVS commits (and is therefore read-only).
Visit http://terror.hungry.com/mailman/listinfo/lesstif-commits (or https version) to subscribe.

Mailing list archives
Both lesstif and lesstif-commits/ are archived online.
Also a second archive of lesstif-commits exists at SF/geocrawler.
Older messages are archived at http://mail-archive.com: for the lesstif and the lesstif-commits lists.
